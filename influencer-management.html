Influencer Management Services

We manage influencer campaigns from sourcing and coordination to execution and performance tracking. Our focus is on aligning brands with the right audiences to ensure campaigns are relevant, controlled, and results driven.

At Nextwave Promotions, we help brands collaborate with the next generation of authentic, relatable influencers. Our influencer management service goes beyond basic matchmaking we create curated partnerships built on trust, relevance, and shared values.

We work with micro- and nano-influencers across South Africa who know how to connect with real audiences and deliver content that performs.

What We Offer

Influencer Sourcing

We match brands with the right influencers based on:

  • Audience demographics and interests
  • Content style and tone
  • Brand fit and reputation
  • Geographic location and engagement rate

Campaign Management

We handle the full influencer campaign cycle:

  • Influencer briefing and onboarding
  • Creative direction and content reviews
  • Scheduling, tracking, and approvals
  • Deadline and deliverable management

Contracting & Compliance

We protect both the brand and the influencer:

  • Usage rights and image permissions
  • Signed agreements with terms & deliverables
  • Payment scheduling and admin support

Content Performance Reports

Get data that matters:

  • Post-campaign engagement metrics
  • Click-throughs, impressions, and reach
  • Insights on influencer audience and sentiment
