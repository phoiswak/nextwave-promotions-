Brand Activation and Field Marketing

Overview

Nextwave Promotions partnered with Mukuru to execute targeted brand activation campaigns aimed at increasing visibility and customer engagement within key communities. The objective was to strengthen awareness of Mukuru’s services through direct, on the ground interaction with potential customers.

Execution

  • Deployed trained field marketing teams in high footfall locations
  • Conducted direct customer engagement and service education
  • Maintained consistent and professional brand representation
  • Supported activations with content capture for digital use

Outcome

  • Increased brand visibility across targeted areas
  • Strong levels of public engagement and interaction
  • Improved awareness of Mukuru’s services within key markets
