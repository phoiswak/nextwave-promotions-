# NextWave Promotions Website

Welcome to the official repository for **NextWave Promotions** — a modern digital platform showcasing brand activations, influencer marketing, content creation, outdoor advertising, and promotional campaigns.

## About the Project

NextWave Promotions is a creative marketing and promotions website designed to showcase services, campaigns, and media content. The platform highlights brand growth strategies and connects businesses with audiences through impactful marketing solutions.

-  Brand Activations showcase
-  Influencer Management services
-  Content Creation portfolio
-  Outdoor Advertising campaigns
-  Image gallery of past work
-  FAQ section
-  Privacy Policy & Terms pages
-  Contact-ready structure for clients
